* Victoria, 20-June-1837 - 22-Jan-1901
* Edward VII, 22-Jan-1901 - 6-May-1910
* George V, 6-May-1910 - 20-Jan-1936
* Edward VII, 20-Jan-1936 - 11-Dec-1936
* George VI, 11-Dec-1936 - 6-Feb-1952
* Elizabeth II, 6-Feb-1952 - present
